About Us

We’re a trio of friends brought together by our shared love for food and hospitality. What started as a passion project in our kitchens has grown into JeeMoNa Catering Services — a heartfelt service built on trust, flavor, and unforgettable experiences.

Each of us brings something unique to the table: from traditional Indian family recipes passed down through generations, to fresh, modern takes on classic dishes. Whether it’s a cozy family gathering, a grand wedding, or a corporate event, we pour our hearts into every meal we serve.

We may have started small, but our dreams (and appetites) are big. And now, we’re here to bring those flavors to your table.
